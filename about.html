Kdrama Alltime

Korean dramas, also known as K-dramas, are popular television series in the Korean language that are made in South Korea. They are popular worldwide, especially in Asia, due to the spread of Korean popular culture. K-dramas cover a wide range of genres, including comedy, romance, sci-fi, and horror. Most K-dramas are based on webcomics, a form of storytelling similar to comics that originated in South Korea.
