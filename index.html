The kiss of cherry blossom in K-dramas is a popular Korean drama trope that involves love blooming under a canopy of cherry blossom trees

Cherry blossoms in Korea are known as beot-kkot or sakura, and they are easy to find. Korean historians claim that Korea has its own native species of cherry tree and that the Japanese razed most of them during their occupation of Korea to plant their own trees.
